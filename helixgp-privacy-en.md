## 9. Your Rights Regarding Personal Data

Under Federal Law No. 152-FZ "On Personal Data," you have the following rights:

### 9.1. Right to Access
You can request information about what personal data the Company processes about you.

### 9.2. Right to Correction

### 9.3. Right to Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
You can request deletion of your personal data in the following cases:
- Processing purposes have been achieved
- You have withdrawn your consent
- Data is being processed unlawfully
- Deletion is required by law

**Limitations:** We cannot delete data if retention is necessary for legal obligations or pending disputes.

### 9.4. Right to Restrict Processing
You can request suspension of data processing in certain cases (e.g., while verifying data accuracy).

### 9.5. Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the legality of processing prior to withdrawal.

### 9.6. Right to Object
You can object to processing of your data if you believe processing is unlawful.

### 9.7. Right to Data Portability
You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (e.g., CSV or JSON) for transfer to another operator.
